<p><ul><li>Free Radical Scavengers</li><li>UV Absorber</li><li>Quencher</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polymer Material Light Stabilizer Market is segmented into several types: Free Radical Scavengers, UV Absorbers, Quenchers, and Others. Free Radical Scavengers protect polymers by neutralizing harmful free radicals generated by UV exposure. UV Absorbers absorb and dissipate UV radiation, preventing degradation. Quenchers work to dissipate energy from excited states, reducing polymer damage. The "Others" category includes various stabilizers that may employ different mechanisms to enhance polymer longevity against light-induced degradation.</p></p>
