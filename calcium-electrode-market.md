<p><strong>The Calcium Electrode Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Membrane</li><li>PVC Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The calcium electrode market comprises two main types: liquid membrane and PVC membrane electrodes. Liquid membrane electrodes utilize a liquid phase containing an ionophore to selectively respond to calcium ions, offering high sensitivity and fast response times. Conversely, PVC membrane electrodes are solid-state devices that incorporate a polyvinyl chloride matrix with a calcium-sensitive compound, providing durability and ease of fabrication. Both types are widely used in various applications, including environmental monitoring and medical diagnostics, reflecting the growing demand for accurate calcium measurement.</p></p>
