<p><p>The stainless steel hospital bed market features three main types: manual, electric, and semi-electric. Manual beds require caregivers to adjust positions using hand cranks, providing a cost-effective solution. Electric beds have powered mechanisms for easy adjustment of position and height, enhancing patient comfort and caregivers' convenience. Semi-electric beds combine both manual and electric features, allowing patients some control while still offering caregiver assistance. Each type caters to different healthcare settings, balancing cost, functionality, and patient needs.</p></p>
