<p><p>The immersible pumps market includes various types catering to specific applications. Non-clog submersible pumps are designed to handle wastewater and solids without clogging, making them ideal for sewage and industrial applications. Openwell submersible pumps are used in open water bodies and shallow wells, efficiently transferring water for irrigation and municipal use. Borewell submersible pumps are constructed for deep wells, delivering water from significant depths for agricultural and drinking purposes. Each type serves unique requirements in fluid management and resource extraction.</p></p>
