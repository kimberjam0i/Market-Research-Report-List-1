<p><strong>The Disk Brakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Opposed Piston Type</li><li>Floating Caliper Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Disk Brakes Market** includes two primary types: **Opposed Piston Type** and **Floating Caliper Type**. Opposed Piston brakes feature pistons on both sides of the rotor, providing balanced force and superior braking performance, ideal for high-performance vehicles. Floating Caliper brakes have pistons on one side, with the caliper sliding to apply pressure evenly, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity, commonly used in standard passenger cars. Both types ensure efficient braking, with Opposed Piston excelling in performance and Floating Caliper prioritizing affordability and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
