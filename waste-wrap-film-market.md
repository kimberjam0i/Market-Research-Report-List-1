<p><p>The waste wrap film market consists primarily of shrink film and stretch film, both utilized for packaging and securing products. Shrink film, which contracts when heat is applied, provides a tight, protective seal around items, making it ideal for retail packaging. Stretch film, on the other hand, is stretched around products to hold them together securely; it offers excellent load stability and visibility. Both types play crucial roles in logistics, ensuring products remain safe during transit and storage while minimizing waste.</p></p>
