Light sensing probes utilize optical technology to detect liquid levels by measuring changes in light transmission. Immersion sensing probes operate by being submerged in the liquid, directly measuring the liquid level through capacitive or resistive means. Other types may include radar or ultrasonic sensing technologies. These probes are critical in industrial, chemical, and wastewater management applications to ensure safety and compliance.</p></p>
