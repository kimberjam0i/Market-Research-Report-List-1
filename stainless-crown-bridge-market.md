They provide structural support and can be made from materials like stainless steel, porcelain, or ceramics. Dental bridges, on the other hand, are prosthetic devices used to replace missing teeth by anchoring to adjacent teeth, ensuring proper alignment and function. Both play crucial roles in improving oral health and overall dental aesthetics.</p></p>
