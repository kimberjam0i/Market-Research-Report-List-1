<p><p>The food preservatives market comprises primarily two types: antimicrobials and antioxidants. Antimicrobials, including substances like sodium benzoate and potassium sorbate, inhibit the growth of bacteria, molds, and yeasts, enhancing food safety and shelf life. Antioxidants, such as ascorbic acid and tocopherols, prevent oxidative damage, maintaining flavor, color, and nutritional value. Together, these preservatives play a crucial role in prolonging food longevity, reducing waste, and ensuring product quality in the food industry, catering to consumer demand for safe and fresh products.</p></p>
