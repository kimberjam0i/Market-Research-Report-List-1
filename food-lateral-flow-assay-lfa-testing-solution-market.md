<p><ul><li>"Sandwich Assays"</li><li>"Competitive Assays"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Food Lateral Flow Assay (LFA) testing solution market comprises primarily two types: Sandwich Assays and Competitive Assays. Sandwich Assays detect target analytes by capturing them between two antibodies, providing high specificity and sensitivity, ideal for quantifying larger molecules like proteins. In contrast, Competitive Assays involve a competition between the target analyte and a labeled antigen for binding sites, often used for small molecules. Both types enhance food safety and quality monitoring by offering rapid, user-friendly detection methods.</p></p>
