<p><p>Induced Air Flotation (IAF) systems are advanced water treatment technologies that utilize the process of buoyancy to remove suspended solids, oils, and other contaminants from wastewater. These systems work by releasing microbubbles into the treatment tank, which attach to the impurities in the water, causing them to float to the surface and form a froth that can be easily removed. </p><p>The IAF system market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing regulatory requirements for water treatment and rising concerns about water pollution.
