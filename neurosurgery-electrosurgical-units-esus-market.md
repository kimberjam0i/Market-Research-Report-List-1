<p><p>Neurosurgery Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are crucial surgical tools used to cut, coagulate, or desiccate tissues during brain surgeries. Monopole ESUs utilize a single electrode for cutting and coagulation, leading to efficient energy delivery over larger areas. Bipolar ESUs have two electrodes, allowing for precise control in delicate surgeries with reduced thermal spread, making them ideal for intricate neural tissue. Vessel Sealing Units specialize in sealing blood vessels, ensuring minimal bleeding and improved patient outcomes during surgical procedures.</p></p>
