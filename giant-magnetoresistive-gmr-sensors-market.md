<p><p>The Giant Magnetoresistive (GMR) sensors market comprises several types, each tailored for specific applications. Standard multilayer sensors utilize alternating layers of magnetic and non-magnetic materials for moderate sensitivity. High-temperature multilayer sensors function effectively in elevated temperature environments. Low hysteresis high-temperature multilayer sensors offer minimal response delays, enhancing performance stability. Spin valve sensors leverage the spin of electrons for high sensitivity. Lastly, the "other" category includes innovative GMR designs that cater to niche applications, expanding the sensor's versatility in various industries.</p></p>
