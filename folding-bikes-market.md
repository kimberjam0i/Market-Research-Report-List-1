<p><p>Folding bikes are designed for portability and space efficiency, with various folding mechanisms catering to different needs. **Mid-fold bikes** split the frame in half, offering compact storage. **Vertical fold bikes** collapse vertically, ideal for tight spaces. **Triangle hinge bikes** use a triangular frame design for stability and quick folding. **Magnet folding bikes** employ magnets to secure folded parts, enhancing convenience. **Suspension system bikes** integrate shock absorption for smoother rides, often combined with folding mechanisms for added versatility. Each type balances portability, functionality, and ride quality.</p></p>
