<p><p>Corrosion and erosion monitoring systems are essential for assessing the integrity of structures, primarily in industrial settings. The market can be categorized into intrusive and non-intrusive types. Intrusive systems involve direct contact with materials, providing precise measurements but potentially damaging the infrastructure. In contrast, non-intrusive systems utilize advanced techniques like ultrasonic or electromagnetic methods, allowing for monitoring without affecting the structure. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety and optimizing maintenance strategies in environments prone to corrosion or erosion.</p></p>
