<p><p>The spectacle lens market primarily includes two main types: single vision spectacle lenses and progressive lenses. Single vision lenses provide a single optical focal point, catering to either near or far vision, making them ideal for individuals with uncomplicated vision requirements. In contrast, progressive lenses offer a gradual transition between multiple focal points, accommodating distance, intermediate, and near vision, thus serving those with presbyopia. Both types play vital roles in enhancing visual clarity and comfort for diverse user needs.</p></p>
