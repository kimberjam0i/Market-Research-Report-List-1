<p><strong>The CPU Heatsink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Cooling CPU Heatsink</li><li>Water Cooling CPU Heatsink</li><li>Thermoelectric Cooling CPU Heatsink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CPU heatsink market consists of three primary types: Air Cooling, Water Cooling, and Thermoelectric Cooling. Air Cooling utilizes aluminum or copper heatsinks with fans to dissipate heat, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Water Cooling employs a liquid coolant system, providing superior thermal management suitable for high-performance applications, albeit at a higher cost. Thermoelectric Cooling utilizes Peltier elements to create a temperature differential, facilitating efficient heat transfer but often requires complex setups and additional power, making it less common.</p></p>
