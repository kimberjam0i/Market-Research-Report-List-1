<p><strong>The Electronystagmography Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Videonystagmography (VNG)</li><li>Rotary Chair Test</li><li>Computerized Dynamic Visual Acuity (DVA)</li><li>Computerized Dynamic Posturography (CDP)</li><li>Subjective Visual Vertical</li><li>Vestibular Evoked Myogenic Potential (VEMP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronystagmography (ENG) testing encompasses various market types for assessing vestibular function. Videonystagmography (VNG) utilizes video technology to analyze eye movements. The Rotary Chair Test measures responses to rotational stimuli. Computerized Dynamic Visual Acuity (DVA) tests visual acuity during head movements, while Computerized Dynamic Posturography (CDP) evaluates balance under dynamic conditions. Subjective Visual Vertical assesses vertical perception, and Vestibular Evoked Myogenic Potential (VEMP) examines auditory-evoked muscle responses. Together, these methods provide comprehensive insights into balance and vestibular disorders.</p></p>
