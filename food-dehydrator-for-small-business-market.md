<p><strong>The Food Dehydrator for Small Business Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Airflow</li><li>Horizontal Airflow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food dehydrators for small businesses come in two main types: vertical airflow and horizontal airflow. Vertical airflow dehydrators have fans located at the top or bottom, allowing heat and air to circulate vertically. This design is typically more compact and cost-effective, suitable for small spaces. In contrast, horizontal airflow dehydrators feature fans on the side, promoting even heat distribution across trays. This results in more uniform drying, making them ideal for businesses that prioritize quality and consistency in their products.</p></p>
