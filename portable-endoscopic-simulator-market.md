<p><p>The Portable Endoscopic Simulator market is categorized into two main types: Portable Flexible Endoscopic Simulators and Portable Rigid Endoscopic Simulators. Portable Flexible Endoscopic Simulators are designed to replicate the use of flexible endoscopes, allowing practitioners to gain skills in navigating the gastrointestinal tract and other flexible procedures. Conversely, Portable Rigid Endoscopic Simulators focus on training for rigid endoscopic techniques used in surgeries like arthroscopy or laparoscopy, providing hands-on practice for precise instrument handling and procedural accuracy.</p></p>
