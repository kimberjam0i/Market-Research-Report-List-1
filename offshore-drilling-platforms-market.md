<p><ul><li>Jack-ups</li><li>Semi-submersibles</li><li>Drillships</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offshore drilling platforms are categorized into three main types: jack-ups, semi-submersibles, and drillships. Jack-ups are mobile platforms with legs that can be jacked up above water; they are ideal for shallow waters. Semi-submersibles float on the surface and are anchored to the seabed, offering stability in deeper waters. Drillships are equipped with drilling equipment and are designed for high-seas operations, capable of moving to different sites. Each type serves specific operational needs and marine conditions.</p></p>
