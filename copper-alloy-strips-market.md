<p><strong>The Copper Alloy Strips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bronze Strip</li><li>Brass Strip</li><li>Cupronickel Strip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copper alloy strips market comprises three main types: bronze strips, brass strips, and cupronickel strips. Bronze strips, primarily made of copper and tin, offer excellent corrosion resistance and are widely used in marine and electrical applications. Brass strips, consisting of copper and zinc, are known for their malleability and conductivity, making them ideal for plumbing and electrical fittings. Cupronickel strips, combining copper with nickel, are valued for their strength and resistance to seawater, commonly used in shipbuilding and cooling systems.</p></p>
