<p><p>The Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD) machines market is primarily segmented into two types: power type and gravity type. Power type machines utilize electricity to facilitate the dialysis process, offering precise control over fluid allocation and timing, thereby enhancing patient convenience and treatment efficacy. In contrast, gravity type machines rely on gravitational force for fluid movement, making them simpler and more cost-effective but potentially less adaptable to complex treatment regimens. Both types cater to varying patient needs and healthcare settings.</p></p>
