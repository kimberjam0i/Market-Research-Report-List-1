<p><p>Sputter coating is a physical vapor deposition (PVD) technique used to apply a thin layer of material onto a substrate, often for the purposes of enhancing appearance, functionality, or protection. It involves ejecting material from a target source, which then deposits onto the substrate, creating a uniform coating. This process is widely utilized in various industries, including electronics, optics, and automotive, due to its versatility and precision.</p><p>The Sputter Coating Market is poised for significant growth, expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.8% during the forecast period.
