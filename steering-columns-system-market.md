<p><p>The steering columns system market comprises three main types: non-adjustable, manually adjustable, and electrically adjustable steering columns. Manually adjustable columns allow drivers to modify the steering wheel's height and angle through physical adjustments, promoting driver comfort. Electrically adjustable steering columns provide advanced convenience, enabling adjustments at the push of a button, often including memory settings for multiple drivers. Each type caters to varying consumer preferences and vehicle designs.</p></p>
