<p><p>Email management software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software offers flexibility and ease of access, allowing users to manage their emails from any device connected to the internet. This model typically entails a subscription fee and includes automatic updates and maintenance. On-premises solutions, on the other hand, are installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control and security, but often requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance by IT staff.</p></p>
