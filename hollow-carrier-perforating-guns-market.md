<p><p>Hollow-carrier perforating guns are essential in oil and gas exploration, utilizing unique designs to create efficient perforation. The WCP (Wireline Conveyed Perforators) type is designed for deployment via wireline, offering flexibility and ease in reaching specific depths. In contrast, TCP (Tubing Conveyed Perforators) types are deployed through tubing, enabling operations in larger wellbore diameters and allowing for higher-pressure scenarios. Each type addresses specific well conditions, optimizing perforation efficiency and enhancing production capabilities.</p></p>
