<p><p>The self-priming twin screw pump market is segmented into double-suction and single-suction types, each serving distinct applications. Double-suction twin-screw pumps feature dual inlets, allowing for higher flow rates and reduced axial loads, making them ideal for larger-scale operations. In contrast, single-suction twin-screw pumps have a single inlet, providing simplicity and efficiency for smaller systems. Both types are valued for their ability to handle viscous fluids, low noise levels, and reliable self-priming capabilities, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
