<p><p>Thickeners, stabilizers, and gelling agents are vital ingredients used in various industries, primarily food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and personal care products. Thickeners increase the viscosity of liquids without substantially altering their other properties, while stabilizers help maintain the uniform dispersion of ingredients, preventing separation. Gelling agents, on the other hand, create a gel-like texture often utilized in products like jellies and desserts.</p><p>The market for thickeners, stabilizers, and gelling agents is undergoing significant growth, driven by rising consumer demand for processed and convenient food products, as well as increasing health consciousness that favors natural and organic ingredients.
