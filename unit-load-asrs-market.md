<p><p>Unit Load Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems (AS/RS) are categorized primarily into single shuttle and dual shuttle systems. Single shuttle systems utilize one shuttle to transport goods to and from storage locations, providing efficient vertical stacking and retrieval within a defined aisle. In contrast, dual shuttle systems employ two shuttles that can operate simultaneously in the same aisle, allowing faster throughput and improved efficiency. Both systems are designed to optimize space utilization and streamline inventory management in warehouses and distribution centers.</p></p>
