<p><p>The tabletop nebulizer market is primarily divided into two types: compressor and non-compressor nebulizers. Compressor nebulizers use a pump to generate a high-velocity airflow that transforms liquid medication into a fine aerosol for inhalation. These devices are effective for various respiratory conditions. On the other hand, non-compressor nebulizers, including ultrasonic and mesh nebulizers, utilize different technologies to deliver medication, often providing quieter operation and portability. Both types cater to diverse patient needs and treatment preferences.</p></p>
