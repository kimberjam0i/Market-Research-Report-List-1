<p><p>The cured formed-in-place gaskets market comprises primarily two types: UV-curable and visible light-curable gaskets. UV-curable gaskets utilize ultraviolet light to initiate a rapid curing process, offering quick production times and high precision, ideal for applications requiring immediate sealing capabilities. In contrast, visible light-curable gaskets are cured using standard visible light sources, providing flexibility in manufacturing and application in environments where UV exposure is undesirable. Both types enhance sealing performance and ensure durability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
