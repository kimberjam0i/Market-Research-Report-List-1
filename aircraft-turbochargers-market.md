<p><ul><li>Butterfly Valve Type</li><li>Poppet Valve Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Turbochargers Market includes two main types: Butterfly Valve and Poppet Valve. Butterfly Valve turbochargers use a rotating disc to regulate airflow, offering precise control and efficiency, ideal for smaller aircraft. Poppet Valve turbochargers employ spring-loaded valves to manage airflow, providing robust performance and durability, often used in larger, high-performance aircraft. Both types enhance engine efficiency at high altitudes, but Butterfly Valves are simpler and lighter, while Poppet Valves handle higher pressures and temperatures, catering to different aircraft needs.</p></p>
