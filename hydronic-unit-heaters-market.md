<p><ul><li>Horizontal Hydronic Unit Heater</li><li>Vertical Hydronic Unit Heater</li><li>Suspended Hydronic Unit Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydronic unit heaters market is segmented into three main types: horizontal, vertical, and suspended hydronic unit heaters. Horizontal hydronic unit heaters are designed to distribute warm air along the floor level, ideal for large spaces. Vertical hydronic unit heaters are space-saving and suited for areas with high ceilings, providing efficient heat distribution upwards. Suspended hydronic unit heaters are mounted from the ceiling, effectively heating open spaces without occupying floor space, making them suitable for industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
