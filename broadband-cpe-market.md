<p><p>Broadband CPE (Customer Premises Equipment) comprises various types that support different internet connectivity methods. Cable CPE utilizes coaxial cables to deliver broadband services, often linked with cable television infrastructure. DSL CPE connects through telephone lines, providing high-speed internet via digital subscriber lines. Fiber-optic CPE employs fiber-optic cables, enabling faster data transmission and higher bandwidth. Additionally, the "Others" category includes satellite and wireless CPE, catering to diverse customer needs in remote or underserved areas, enhancing overall broadband accessibility.</p></p>
