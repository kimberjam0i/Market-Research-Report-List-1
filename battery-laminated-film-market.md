<p><p>The Battery Laminated Film Market is divided into two primary types: Thermal Process and Dry Process. The Thermal Process involves applying heat to bond the film layers, ensuring strong adhesion and improved barrier properties. This method is often favored for its efficiency and durability. In contrast, the Dry Process utilizes adhesive technologies at room temperature, providing a solvent-free alternative. Both processes enhance the performance and longevity of battery packs, catering to diverse applications in the energy storage sector.</p></p>
