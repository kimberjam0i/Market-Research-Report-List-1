<p><p>The brain tumor diagnosis and treatments market is segmented into primary and secondary brain tumors. Primary brain tumors originate in the brain, such as gliomas and meningiomas, and require specialized diagnostic techniques like MRI and CT scans, alongside targeted therapies and surgeries. Secondary brain tumors, or metastatic tumors, arise from cancers elsewhere in the body, necessitating a focus on systemic treatments such as chemotherapy and radiation. Both markets involve advancements in early detection and personalized treatment options to improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
