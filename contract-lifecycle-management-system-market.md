<p><p>Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM) Systems are categorized into two main market types: Cloud-Based and On-Premise. Cloud-Based CLM solutions offer flexibility and scalability, allowing organizations to access contracts from anywhere and reducing the need for extensive IT infrastructure.
