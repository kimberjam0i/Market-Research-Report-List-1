<p><p>The hydrogen storage technology market is categorized into three main types: physical hydrogen storage, chemical hydrogen storage, and other methods. </p><p>Physical hydrogen storage includes high-pressure gas cylinders and cryogenic tanks that store hydrogen in liquid or gaseous forms. Chemical hydrogen storage involves reversible reactions with materials, such as metal hydrides or chemical compounds, to absorb and release hydrogen. Other market types may include hybrid systems or innovative approaches like bio-hydrogen processes, expanding the versatility and efficiency of hydrogen storage solutions across various applications.</p></p>
