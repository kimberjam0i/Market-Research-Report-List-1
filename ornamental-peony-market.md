Conversely, Paeonia lactiflora, or herbaceous peonies, is characterized by its softer stems and diverse colors, flourishing in various environments. Both types are popular in gardens and floral arrangements, appealing to horticulturists and consumers alike due to their beauty and variety of forms.</p></p>
