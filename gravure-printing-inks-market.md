<p><p>The Gravure Printing Inks market includes solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based inks use organic solvents as carriers, offering fast drying, vibrant colors, and durability, making them ideal for packaging and labels. However, they emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs), raising environmental concerns. Water-based inks use water as a carrier, reducing VOC emissions and being more eco-friendly. They are suitable for food packaging and publications but may require longer drying times and specialized substrates. Both types cater to diverse printing needs, balancing performance and sustainability.</p></p>
