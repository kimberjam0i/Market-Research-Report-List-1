<p><p>The LED traffic signals market primarily consists of two types based on energy sources: electric power and solar energy. Electric power signals are directly connected to the grid, offering continuous operation and reliability, making them suitable for urban areas with stable electricity supply. In contrast, solar-powered LED signals utilize photovoltaic panels to harness sunlight, providing an eco-friendly alternative with lower operational costs, ideal for remote or off-grid locations. Both types enhance traffic management and promote energy efficiency.</p></p>
