<p><p>The compact fuel cell market is primarily categorized into four types: Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cells (PEMFCs), Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFC), Molten Carbonate Fuel Cells (MCFC), and Direct Methanol Fuel Cells (DMFC). PEMFCs are favored for portable applications due to their quick start and low operating temperature. SOFCs offer high efficiency and versatility for stationary use. MCFCs operate at high temperatures and are suitable for large-scale energy production, while DMFCs are ideal for portable devices, utilizing methanol directly for energy conversion.</p></p>
