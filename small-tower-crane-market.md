<p><ul><li>Self-erecting Tower Cranes</li><li>Flat Top Tower Cranes</li><li>Hammerhead Tower Cranes</li><li>Luffing Jib Tower Cranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small tower crane market features several key types, each serving distinct construction needs. Self-erecting tower cranes are compact and can be assembled on-site, ideal for smaller projects. Flat top tower cranes have a flat upper structure, offering unobstructed lifting, suitable for urban environments. Hammerhead tower cranes provide powerful lifting capabilities with a horizontal jib, favored for larger projects. Luffing jib tower cranes save space on congested sites by allowing the jib to swing closer to the tower, enhancing maneuverability.</p></p>
