<p><p>Ceramic fiber boards are lightweight, high-temperature insulation materials made from ceramic fibers bonded with inorganic binders. These boards are widely used in furnace linings, kilns, and other high-temperature environments due to their durability and energy efficiency.</p><p>The Ceramic Fiber Boards Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.3% during the forecast period.
