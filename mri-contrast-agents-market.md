<p><p>MRI contrast agents are substances used to enhance the visibility of internal structures in magnetic resonance imaging (MRI). They help differentiate between abnormal and normal tissues, aiding in the diagnosis of various medical conditions. These agents are typically based on gadolinium, a paramagnetic metal, which affects the relaxation times of nearby protons, resulting in improved image contrast.</p><p>The MRI Contrast Agents Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for advanced imaging techniques in healthcare. Factors contributing to this growth include the rising prevalence of chronic diseases, an aging population, and advancements in MRI technology that improve diagnostic accuracy. Additionally, the development of new contrast agents with fewer side effects and better safety profiles is enhancing patient outcomes and satisfaction.</p><p>Emerging trends include a growing focus on personalized medicine, leading to tailored imaging solutions.
