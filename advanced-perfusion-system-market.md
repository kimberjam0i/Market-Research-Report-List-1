<p><p>The Advanced Perfusion System market is primarily divided into two types of heart-lung machines: Single Roller Pump and Double Roller Pump systems. Single Roller Pump machines utilize one roller pump to circulate blood and maintain perfusion during cardiac surgery, offering simplicity and lower costs. In contrast, Double Roller Pump machines employ two roller pumps for enhanced flow control and redundancy, improving patient safety and efficiency. Both systems play vital roles in supporting cardiopulmonary functions during surgical procedures.</p></p>
