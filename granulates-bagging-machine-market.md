<p><p>The granulates bagging machine market features two primary types: vertical and horizontal bagging machines. Vertical bagging machines are designed for filling and sealing bags from the bottom to the top, ideal for lightweight granules and offering faster operation for high-speed packaging. In contrast, horizontal bagging machines operate with the bags placed horizontally, suitable for bulkier items and more complex packaging requirements. Both types enhance efficiency in packaging processes across industries, catering to diverse product specifications and production needs.</p></p>
