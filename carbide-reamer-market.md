<p><ul><li>Monobloc Type</li><li>Insert Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbide Reamer Market includes two primary types: Monobloc Type and Insert Type. Monobloc reamers are made from a single piece of carbide, offering high precision, rigidity, and durability, ideal for consistent, high-accuracy applications. Insert Type reamers feature replaceable carbide tips, providing cost-effectiveness and flexibility, as worn inserts can be replaced without discarding the entire tool. Both types cater to industries requiring precise hole finishing, with Monobloc excelling in stability and Insert Type offering adaptability and reduced long-term costs.</p></p>
