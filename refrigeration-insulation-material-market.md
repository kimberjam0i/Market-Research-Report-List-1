<p><p>The refrigeration insulation material market comprises several types, each offering unique benefits. Elastomeric foam provides excellent flexibility and moisture resistance, making it ideal for various applications. Polyurethane (PU) and Polyisocyanurate (PIR) offer high thermal resistance and durability. Polystyrene foam is lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for various insulation needs. Fiberglass is known for its thermal performance and fire resistance. Phenolic foam provides low thermal conductivity and is often used in specialized settings. Other materials include cellulose and reflective insulation, catering to diverse requirements.</p></p>
