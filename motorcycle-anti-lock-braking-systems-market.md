<p><p>Motorcycle Anti-lock Braking Systems (ABS) enhance safety by preventing wheel lock during braking. **Single-channel ABS** operates on one wheel, typically the front, offering basic anti-lock functionality at a lower cost. It's common in smaller or budget-friendly motorcycles. **Multi-channel ABS** works on both wheels, providing advanced control and stability, especially in varied road conditions. It's preferred in high-performance or premium motorcycles, offering superior safety and braking efficiency. Both types cater to different market segments, balancing cost and performance needs.</p></p>
