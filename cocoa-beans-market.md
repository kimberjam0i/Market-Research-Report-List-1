<p><ul><li>Criollo Cocoa Beans</li><li>Forastero Cocoa Beans</li><li>Trinitario Cocoa Beans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cocoa bean market can be categorized into three main types: Criollo, Forastero, and Trinitario. Criollo beans are prized for their unique flavor and aroma, making them sought-after for premium chocolate products. Forastero beans dominate the market due to their higher yield and robust flavor, suitable for mass production. Trinitario beans, a hybrid of the other two, offer a balance of quality and yield, appealing to both artisanal and commercial chocolate makers. Each type plays a vital role in the global cocoa industry.</p></p>
