<p><p>The IoT RFID readers market is categorized into three main types: Low Frequency (LF) readers, High Frequency (HF) readers, and Ultra High Frequency (UHF) readers. LF readers operate at frequencies around 125-134 kHz, suitable for short-range applications like animal tracking. HF readers, typically operating at 13.56 MHz, are used for smart cards and access control. UHF readers operate between 860-960 MHz, allowing for longer read ranges and enabling applications in supply chain management and inventory tracking, offering greater versatility and efficiency.</p></p>
