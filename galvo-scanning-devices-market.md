<p><p>Galvo scanning devices are precision optical systems utilized for directing laser beams in various applications. The market is categorized into single-axis, dual-axis, and tri-axis types. Single-axis devices control beam movement along one direction, making them suitable for simpler tasks. Dual-axis systems allow for two-dimensional scanning, enhancing coverage and flexibility. Tri-axis devices provide full three-dimensional movement, enabling complex applications like 3D laser engraving and cutting. Each type serves distinct industries, from material processing to medical imaging and precision machining.</p></p>
